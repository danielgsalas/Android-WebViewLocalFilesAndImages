Photo Policy from the Tournament FAQ:

There is no commercial photography allowed at the Tournament of the Phoenix. All photos & videos taken at the tournament may only be used for personal use unless authorization is granted in writing by WorldJoust Tournaments™. In all cases, the reproduction rights to all images of competitors and participants is retained by the individual and may not be sold without their express written permission. Photographers who attempt to sell photographs of event participants will be prosecuted to the full extent of the law.

Disclaimer: This mobile app and the associated tutorial are not for sale, and neither are the included photographs and videos. Therefore I do not believe they are in violation of the above photo policy. Thank you.
